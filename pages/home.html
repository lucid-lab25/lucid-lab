Welcome to LUCID Lab

LUCID (Learning and Usability for Cybersecurity and Intelligent Decisions) Lab, led by Dr. Khandakar Ashrafi Akbar, explores how AI and generative technologies can transform security, privacy, and decision-making. Guided by the vision of “Accessible by All Security”, we design defenses that are usable, scalable, and impactful for real-world adoption.


Our work spans human-centered and usable security, exploring the risks and opportunities in generative AI, building context-aware intelligent systems, designing transparent and resilient evaluation metrics, and advancing the performance analysis of next-generation AI models.
