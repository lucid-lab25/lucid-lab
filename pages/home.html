Introduction

Lucid Lab, led by Professor Khandakar Ashrafi Akbar, explores how artificial general intelligence (AGI), machine learning, and generative AI can transform security and privacy. Our research develops usable, scalable, and sustainable defenses—from automated patch recommendation systems to system-level protections—guided by the vision of “Accessible by All Security.” We focus on bridging cutting-edge AI with real-world impact, ensuring that intelligent technologies remain secure, inclusive, and environmentally responsible.
