Introduction

LUCID Lab (Learning and Usability for Cybersecurity and Intelligent Decisions), led by Dr. Khandakar Ashrafi Akbar, investigates how artificial general intelligence (AGI), machine learning, and generative AI can transform the landscape of security, privacy, and intelligent decision-making. Guided by the vision of “Accessible by All Security”, our mission is to design defenses that are not only usable, scalable, and sustainable, but also meaningful for real-world adoption.


Our research spans a diverse set of areas, including:

  • Human-Centered and Usable Security – ensuring systems are secure while remaining intuitive and inclusive for all users.
  • Exploration of Nuanced Factors in Generative AI Systems – uncovering hidden biases, risks, and opportunities within advanced AI models.
  • Development of Context-Aware Intelligent Systems – building adaptive technologies that make secure, informed decisions in dynamic environments.
  • Design of Resilient and Interpretable Evaluation Metrics – creating transparent methods to measure the robustness and fairness of intelligent systems.
  • Performance Evaluation and Analysis of Generative AI Models – advancing rigorous frameworks for testing and optimizing next-generation AI.
